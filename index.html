Cover Letter

Ecuadorian athlete and scientist with a focus on bioinformatics, based in Bochum, Germany. In 2023, I completed my Bachelor’s degree in Biochemistry at Ruhr University Bochum (RUB), and I am now in the final semester of my Master’s program in Molecular and Developmental Stem Cell Biology. I am currently writing my Master’s thesis in the Multidimensional Omics Analysis (MdOA) research group at the Leibniz Institute for Analytical Sciences – ISAS – in Dortmund, where I also work as a student research assistant. My project involves curating a Short-Scale Metabolic Model (Mitocore), integrating proteomics data, and comparing it with well-annotated Large-Scale Models. Additionally, I am implementing automated model updates following CI/CD principles. A little about me: In addition to my native language, Spanish, I am proficient in German and English (C1–C2 level). Although I no longer compete, sports remain a central part of my life.
